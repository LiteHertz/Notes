French Canadian vs English Canadian formatting

Number, date, time, and currency conventions compared

1,000,000		 FR groups with a thin space; EN groups with a comma.

75%		 FR follows the French typographic rule of a space before the symbol.

Negative currency Minus before the amount

-$12.50 or ($12.50)		 EN commonly uses parentheses in accounting contexts; FR does not.
